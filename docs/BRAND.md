# Sparebox Brand Identity

---

## 1. Brand Purpose & Values

### Purpose
**Sparebox exists to democratize AI infrastructure** — letting anyone monetize idle hardware and anyone deploy AI agents without wrestling with complex cloud setup.

### Values (3)

**1. Simplicity**
Complex under the hood, dead simple on the surface. Deploy an agent in minutes. Register a machine in minutes. No DevOps degree required.

**2. Trustworthiness**
Reliable uptime. Transparent pricing. Fair 60/40 revenue split. Your agent runs, your machine earns, no surprises.

**3. Efficiency**
Every idle CPU cycle is wasted potential. We turn dormant hardware into productive infrastructure. We respect your time — no bloat, no friction.

---

## 2. Brand Personality

If Sparebox were a person at a party, they'd be the **sharp, approachable tech founder** — the one who built something cool and explains it without making you feel dumb. Confident but never arrogant. Uses plain words for complex ideas. Probably wearing a simple black t-shirt.

### Personality Spectrum
- Serious ↔ **Playful** (slightly playful, not silly)
- Formal ↔ **Casual** (casual but competent)
- Reserved ↔ **Confident** (confident, direct)
- Traditional ↔ **Modern** (modern, forward-looking)
- Understated ↔ **Bold** (balanced, clean impact)

## 3. Voice & Tone

### Voice (Always)
**Clear. Confident. Human.**

### Voice Rules

| Trait | Always Do | Never Do |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| **Clear** | Use plain language. If a smart 16-year-old couldn't understand it, rewrite it. | Use jargon like "leverage," "synergy," "utilize," or "scalable solution." |
| **Confident** | State facts directly. "Deploy in 2 minutes." | Hedge with weak language. "You could potentially deploy in as little as..." |
| **Human** | Write like you're talking to a technical friend. Contractions okay. | Sound like a corporate press release or legal document. |

### Tone by Context

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Marketing | Energetic, benefit-forward | "Your old laptop could be earning $50/month right now." |
| Dashboard | Clean, informative, minimal | "3 agents running. 99.8% uptime this month." |
| Error states | Helpful, never blaming | "Connection lost. We're trying to reconnect — check your host's status." |
| Success moments | Warm, brief | "Agent deployed. It's live." |
| Pricing | Transparent, confident | "$12/month. Host gets $7.20. We get $4.80. Simple." |

### Voice Examples

**Announcing a feature:**
- ❌ Generic: "We are pleased to announce the release of our new real-time monitoring functionality, designed to provide users with enhanced visibility."
- ✅ Sparebox: "Real-time monitoring is live. See exactly what your agent's doing, right now."

**Error message:**
- ❌ Generic: "An error has occurred. Please try again later or contact support."
- ✅ Sparebox: "Something broke on our end. We're looking into it. Try again in a minute?"

## 5. Typography

### Font Family
**Inter** — Clean, modern, highly readable at all sizes. Perfect for both UI and marketing.

```css
font-family: 'Inter', -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', sans-serif;
```

### Type Scale

| Element | Size | Weight | Letter Spacing |
|---------|------|--------|----------------|
| H1 (Hero) | 48-72px | 700 (Bold) | -0.02em |
| H2 (Section) | 36-48px | 600 (Semibold) | -0.01em |
| H3 (Card title) | 24-30px | 600 (Semibold) | 0 |
| Body | 16-18px | 400 (Regular) | 0 |
| Small/Caption | 14px | 400 (Regular) | 0 |
| Code | 14px | 400 (Mono) | 0 |

### Font Rules
- Headlines: Bold or Semibold only. Never light.
- Body: Regular weight. Bold for emphasis, sparingly.
- Code: Use `font-mono` (JetBrains Mono or similar)

## 7. Imagery Style

### Aesthetic
- **Dark, minimal, geometric**
- Subtle depth with shadows and glows
- Abstract representations of networks, connections, and compute
- No stock photos of people
- Illustrations over photography when possible

### Visual Elements
- Gradient glows (violet → cyan)
- Subtle grid patterns (representing infrastructure)
- Rounded rectangles (modern, friendly)
- Soft shadows and depth
- Dot patterns for texture

### Iconography
- Lucide icons (consistent, clean)
- 24px default size
- 1.5-2px stroke weight
- Match text color (muted or primary as appropriate)

---

## 8. Brand Decision Filter

Before publishing anything, ask:

1. **Does this reflect our values?** (Simple? Trustworthy? Efficient?)
2. **Does this sound like us?** (Clear? Confident? Human?)
3. **Does this look like us?** (Dark mode? Violet/cyan? Clean type?)
4. **Would a technical person trust this?** (No fluff? Substance over style?)

If any answer is no, revise.
